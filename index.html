Questions

Everything you need to know.

Is this HIPAA-compliant?+
Yes. We sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with every practice. Patient data is de-identified in our system — we store only hashed patient references, CDT codes, and dollar amounts. No names, no DOBs, no SSNs stored on our servers.
How do I export claims data from my practice management software?+
All major PM systems support this. In Dentrix: Reports → Management → Insurance Payment Summary → Export CSV. In Eaglesoft: Reports → Insurance → Payment Report → Export. In Open Dental: Reports → Monthly → Insurance Payment → Export CSV. Exact steps depend on your version — we have a one-page guide for each PM system inside the dashboard.
When do you charge your fee?+
We charge 18% only on amounts actually recovered and confirmed by you. When a payer sends additional payment, you log it in the dashboard. We invoice at month-end based on what was confirmed recovered. Zero fee on flagged claims that weren't recovered. Zero monthly platform fee on the Recovery plan.
Do you submit appeals to insurers directly?+
No — and that's intentional. Insurer portals don't have public APIs, and auto-submitting on your behalf creates liability. Instead, we draft a complete, specific appeal letter ready to send. Your billing staff reviews it (typically takes 2 minutes) and submits through your normal channel. This keeps you in control and keeps us compliant.
Which insurance companies do you work with?+
All of them. Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, United Concordia, Humana, Guardian, BlueCross — any payer that uses standard CDT codes and sends remittance data (835/EOB). We use CARC/RARC denial codes which are industry-standard across all commercial payers.
How long does the first audit take?+
Upload takes 30 seconds. Analysis typically completes within 2–4 hours. For practices with over 500 claims/month, allow up to 24 hours for the first full audit. After setup, ongoing monitoring is continuous — new claims analyzed within hours of upload.
